About Me

I grew up in Chanhassen Minnesota and have lived in Minnesota my whole life. I also grew up with a large family, with me being one of 6 kids, that I love. Since going to college I have discovered a love for web development and building websites and would love to help you get the website you want, the way you imagined it.

I also have a dog named Filbert, he is a black mouth cur, who has the energy of a thousand suns! As of when I’m writing this, he is one year old and will hopefully loose some of that energy when he gets older, sometimes its too much! Me and my brother adopted him from a rescue service and he has been part of the family ever since.

I love D&D and all things fantasy! Ever since I was a child I was always taken in by Tolkien and other large fantasy writers. So when I DM , dungeon master, it sort of feels like I get to be Tolkien and tell an epic fantasy story that hopefully my players enjoy!
